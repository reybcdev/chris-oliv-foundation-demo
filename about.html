About Our Mission

Building brighter futures through education, one classroom at a time.

Our Mission

Chris-Oliv Foundation improves learning outcomes for children in underserved communities by rehabilitating school facilities, establishing reading spaces, and supplying books and teacher resources.

We believe that every child deserves access to a safe, inspiring learning environment equipped with the resources they need to succeed.
