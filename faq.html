Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs, donations, and impact.

The Chris-Oliv Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving education in underserved communities through our three-pillar approach: Fix (classroom infrastructure), Fuel (books and learning materials), and Follow-through (training and ongoing support).

85% of every donation goes directly to our programs and services. The remaining 15% covers essential administrative costs and fundraising activities that enable us to continue and expand our work.

We prioritize Title I schools and those serving high-need communities. Schools can apply to work with us, or we may reach out based on community referrals. We assess each school's needs and readiness to maintain improvements long-term.

Yes, the Chris-Oliv Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law, and we provide receipts for all contributions.
