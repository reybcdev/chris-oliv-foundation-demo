How We Measure Impact

📊 Outputs

Direct deliverables we track

  • Projects completed
  • Books delivered
  • Teacher sessions held
  • Reading minutes logged

📈 Outcomes (Lightweight)

Changes we monitor

  • Attendance trends
  • Library checkouts
  • Teacher feedback
  • Community engagement

Our Approach: We believe in lightweight, practical measurement that doesn't burden schools while providing clear evidence of impact. Our methodology focuses on sustainable data collection that schools can maintain independently.
